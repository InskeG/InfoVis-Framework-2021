Introduction

Our job is to make YOUR wildest visualisations come true and give you insight into the most popular trends currently on spofity. We focus on providing multiple ways to investigate these trends. All these visualisations we performed with HTML, CSS and Javascript to provide the user with the required interactivity necessary to change the visualisations according to the users prefreences. Moreover, it also provides a template for others who are interested in diving deeper into the spotify dataset that was obtained from Kaggle. The sky is the limit and we hope to inspire others in data analysis!
